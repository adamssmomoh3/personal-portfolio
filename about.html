About Me

My personal life story and experience

My name is Sahr Adams Momoh, and I am a proud Sierra Leonean, hailing from the vibrant Eastern Region, specifically the heart of Kono District. My educational journey is one I cherish deeply, as it shaped the foundation of who I am today. It all began at my mother's cherished school, F A W A Primary School, located in the lively town of Koidu City, where I attended from Class 1 to 3. I later continued my studies at Baptist Primary School for Class 3 to 4 and then at First Baptist Primary School from Class 5 to 6. It was at this final step of my primary education that I successfully sat for the NPSE exam, marking a significant milestone in my academic path.

“Every step forward is a foundation for the future.”

My journey through junior secondary education was both enriching and formative. I attended the esteemed Unique International Secondary School from J.S.S. 1 to 2, and then transitioned to the historic Koidu Secondary School from J.S.S. 2 to 3, where I successfully completed my BECE exam.

Building on this foundation, I pursued my senior secondary education with dedication, advancing from S.S.S. 1 to 3. This chapter of my academic life culminated in the successful completion of the prestigious WASSCE exam, marking the fulfillment of my high school journey.

“Education is the compass that guides my aspirations and fuels my dreams.”
